# Bike Sales Excel Dashboard

An Excel-based **interactive** dashboard for analyzing bike buyers and understanding the customer segments most likely to purchase bikes.

## Project File

- **Excel Project Dashboard.xlsx** – Main dashboard workbook containing the bike sales analysis and visualizations.

## Overview

This project uses Excel to clean, analyze, and visualize bike sales data, focusing on customer demographics, commute patterns, income levels, regions, and occupations. The dashboard helps identify which customer segments are more likely to purchase bikes and why.

## Key Insights

- Middle-aged customers are more likely to purchase bikes compared to younger and older age groups.
- Customers with short commute distances (0–5 miles) show higher bike purchase rates.
- Bike purchases are not limited to high-income customers; lifestyle and commuting needs play a major role.
- Certain regions show stronger demand for bikes than others.
- Customers with fewer cars and specific occupations are more inclined to purchase bikes.

For more detailed findings, see [`INSIGHTS.md`](./INSIGHTS.md).

## How to Use

1. Download the file **Excel Project Dashboard.xlsx**.
2. Open it in Microsoft Excel (desktop) for full slicer and chart interactivity.
3. Use filters for:
   - Age group
   - Commute distance
   - Region
   - Income
   - Occupation
4. Observe how the charts update to explore different customer segments and bike purchase patterns.
